Olonana Nkera

Our Treatment Center

Olonana Nkera Treatment Center is committed to addressing a pressing social issue in Kiambu County: the high prevalence of drug and alcohol abuse.

According to the latest 2023 survey by the National Campaign Against Drug Abuse (NACADA), this region faces significant public rehabilitation challenges.

Our treatment center aims to be a beacon of hope and transformation, providing a comprehensive and compassionate approach to treatment.

Our center is dedicated to offering personalized treatment plans, support, and care to individuals struggling with addiction.

Using effective therapies and a holistic approach, we help people recover and regain control of their lives.

What We Do

Here at Olonana Nkera Treatment Center

We offer complete addiction programs that are designed to meet the needs of people with substance abuse issues.

We also use proven therapies, personalized care, and holistic methods to support healing and recovery.

Who We Are

A Place of Hope

Olonana Nkera, meaning "child of hope" in Maasai, reflects our commitment to providing hope to individuals struggling with substance abuse disorders and their families. At Olonana Nkera Treatment Center, therapy sessions are conducted by dedicated addiction counselors and therapists who are focused on delivering compassionate, individualized care.

Our approach includes:

  • Compassionate Care:
    • We provide caring support tailored to each person’s needs, making sure everyone feels valued and understood.
  • Community Support:
    • Our center helps clients feel like they belong, build lasting relationships, and find strength in a supportive community.
  • Holistic Environment:
    • We create a caring space that supports emotional, mental, and physical well-being, which is key for a successful recovery.
  • Family Involvement:
    • We involve families in recovery by offering counseling and support to strengthen relationships and build a strong foundation for lasting recovery.

Our dedicated team is committed to walking alongside each individual, offering unwavering support and encouragement throughout their recovery journey. Together, we work to restore hope, strengthen bonds, and transform lives.

Our Services

At Olonana Nkera Treatment Center, we provide evidence-based treatment programs to help individuals overcome addiction. Our approach combines proven practices with personalized care to support long-term recovery. Our programs include:

INDIVIDUAL THERAPY

Our individual therapy sessions offer a private space for clients to address personal challenges. Guided by experienced therapists, clients explore the root causes of addiction, develop coping strategies, and work toward recovery using methods like cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), motivational interviewing, and trauma-informed therapy.

GROUP THERAPY

Our group therapy sessions create a supportive community where clients share experiences and support each other. Led by skilled therapists, these sessions help build interpersonal skills, increase self-awareness, and promote collective recovery.

MEDICAL DETOXIFICATION (IF NEEDED)

For clients needing detoxification, we provide 24/7 supervision and care to manage withdrawal symptoms. Our medical team monitors clients closely, offering medications and support to ensure a safe and effective detox process. This important first step sets the stage for the recovery journey ahead.

EDUCATION AND SKILL BUILDING

We offer workshops and classes such as Bakery,Masonry,Carpentry,Craftsmansip,Gardening and Landscaping to educate clients about addiction and recovery. Clients also learn essential life skills like stress management and communication, which are crucial for maintaining sobriety and a balanced life. These sessions provide the knowledge and tools needed to face life's challenges successfully.

RELAPSE PREVENTION

We focus on relapse prevention by teaching clients how to handle triggers, cravings, and high-risk situations. Using strategies like cognitive-behavioral techniques, mindfulness, and coping skills training, clients build resilience to stay committed to sobriety. Ongoing support helps them manage post-treatment challenges.

AFTERCARE

Our support continues after clients leave the center. We offer aftercare planning, including ongoing counseling, support group referrals, alumni programs, and community resources. These services help clients maintain recovery, build support networks, and continue personal growth.

FAITH-BASED INTERVENTION

Our faith-based program integrates spiritual practices into recovery. We offer spiritual counseling, prayer groups, and faith-based support to help clients find strength and hope in their spiritual journey. This approach can support addiction recovery and long-term sobriety.
